About County Services Portal

Modernizing county government services through digital transformation

Our Mission

To provide efficient, transparent, and accessible county services through innovative digital solutions that serve our communities better.

Our Vision

To be the leading example of digital government services, making county administration more responsive and citizen-centered.

What We Offer

Online Applications

Submit permit applications, license renewals, and other requests online from anywhere, anytime.

Application Tracking

Track the status of your applications in real-time and receive updates throughout the review process.

Multi-Role Access

Different access levels for citizens, county staff, and administrators ensure appropriate permissions and workflow.

Secure & Reliable

Built with security best practices and reliable infrastructure to protect your data and ensure service availability.

Faster Processing

Streamlined workflows and automated processes reduce processing times and improve service delivery.

Mobile Friendly

Access services from any device with our responsive design that works on desktop, tablet, and mobile.

System Architecture

Technology Stack

  • Backend: Python Flask with SQLAlchemy
  • Frontend: Bootstrap 5 with responsive design
  • Database: SQLite (development) / PostgreSQL (production)
  • Authentication: Flask-Security-Too
  • Authorization: Role-based access control (RBAC)
